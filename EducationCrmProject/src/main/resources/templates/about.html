About CampusConnect

Streamlining Course Sales, Learning, and Administration

What is CampusConnect?

CampusConnect is a full-stack web application designed to manage the complete lifecycle of online course sales and student learning experiences.

Our platform integrates Razorpay payment gateway for secure transactions and provides three distinct role-based interfaces tailored for Users, Administrators, and Employees.

Mission: To provide an all-in-one solution for educational institutions to manage students, courses, employees, and sales analytics in real-time.
